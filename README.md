Jordan Water-Guard: AI Price Transparency Tool The ProblemIn Jordan, many households rely on private water tankers during summer shortages or maintenance on the Disi water pipe. This market often lacks transparency, leading to:Price Gouging: Drivers charging double the fair rate in high-demand areas.Information Gap: Citizens don't know the "fair market price" based on distance and fuel costs.Trust Issues: Difficulty in distinguishing between licensed and unofficial sources. The Solution : This project is an AI-powered assistant designed for the Jordanian public. It uses a Machine Learning model to predict a "Fair Market Price" for water delivery, empowering citizens to negotiate fairly and avoid scams. Technical FeaturesPredictive Engine: A Random Forest Regressor trained on 10,000 synthetic records modeled after 2026 Jordanian water tariffs and fuel costs.Dynamic Inputs: The model accounts for Location (District), Tanker Size (m^3), Distance from Well, and Seasonal Crisis markers.Lightweight UI: Built with Streamlit for a mobile-first experience, allowing users to check prices instantly on their phones while standing at their front door.Accuracy: Achieved a Mean Absolute Error (MAE) of 1.11 JOD, providing highly reliable estimates for the local market. Project Structureapp.py: The user-facing dashboard.price_predictor_model.pkl: The trained AI "brain."district_encoder.joblib: The translator for Jordanian district names.train_price_model.py: The script used to train and validate the model.This tool moves AI out of the "IT bubble" and into the streets of Jordan. It provides a non-technical, bilingual solution to a daily life challenge, proving that AI can be a tool for social fairness.
